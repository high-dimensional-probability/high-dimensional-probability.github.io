## (a)
Follows immediately from (b).

## (b)
If $B - A \ge 0$, then clearly $Q(B - A)Q \ge 0$.

## (c)
Use (a) and (b) to compute
\begin{equation}
    \E[Q^j]
    = \E[Q Q^{j - 2} Q]
    \le b^{j-2} \E[Q^2]
    = b^{j-2} \Var(Q).
\end{equation}
