Denote $$Z_n = \max_{i \in [n]}\abs{X_i}$$.
Use that $$x \mapsto \psi(\sigma^{-1} x)$$ is strictly increasing and convex:
\begin{equation}
    \psi(\sigma^{-1}\E[Z_n])
    \le \E[\psi(\sigma^{-1}Z_n)]
    \le \sum_{i=1}^n \E[\psi(\sigma^{-1}\abs{X_i})]
    \le n,
\end{equation}
so
\begin{equation}
    \E[Z_n] \le \sigma \psi^{-1}(n).
\end{equation}
