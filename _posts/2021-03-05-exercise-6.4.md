Take the eigendecomposition $$A = Q \Lambda Q^\top$$, and observe
$$I + A = Q (I + \Lambda) Q^\top \, .$$ Since $$1 + x \leq e^x$$
\begin{align}
  I + A
  \preceq
  Q e^{\Lambda} Q^\top
  =
  e^{A}
  \, .
\end{align}
